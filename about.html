DAY6

A Musical Journey of Emotion and Authenticity

Our Story

Formed under JYP Entertainment in 2015, DAY6 has carved a unique path in the K-rock scene. Known for their deeply emotional lyrics and authentic sound, the band has consistently pushed boundaries in Korean pop music.

Sungjin

Lead Vocalist & Guitarist

The band's emotional core, Sungjin's powerful vocals and introspective songwriting define DAY6's signature sound.

Young K

Bassist & Rapper

A lyrical genius, Young K's rap and writing skills bring depth and complexity to the band's music.

Wonpil

Keyboard & Vocalist

With his angelic voice and keyboard skills, Wonpil adds a layer of emotional vulnerability to DAY6.

Dowoon

Drummer & Vocalist

The rhythmic backbone of the band, Dowoon's drumming and increasingly prominent vocals complete DAY6's sound.
